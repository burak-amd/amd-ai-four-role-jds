AMD AI — Four role descriptions

Draft JDs derived from voice-of-customer, partnerships RL, hardware AI research, and applied priorities themes.

1 · Forward Deployed AI Researcher Scale voice of the customer and the technical–business bridge internally and for strategic deals. 2 · AI Research Scientist, RL (Strategic partnerships) RL for customer collaborations on kernel, PPA, and code optimization; reward hacking and partnership research. 3 · AI Research Scientist (Hardware engineering & reward design) Metareasoning, reward shaping, and high or mixed-latency rewards for internal HW and generalization. 4 · Applied AI Engineer (Top hardware priorities) RTL tooling, acceleration programs, PPA scale-out, and RSI for compute across product generations.
