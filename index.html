AMD AI — Four research role descriptions

https://burak-amd.github.io/amd-ai-four-role-jds/ — AI Research Scientist (RL, RSI, generalizing hardware engineering) and AI Research Engineer (RL infra).

1 · AI Research Scientist, RL Reinforcement learning algorithms and post-training for generative AI on challenging engineering and silicon-adjacent tasks. 2 · AI Research Scientist, RSI Recursive self-improvement: bounded improvement loops, self-training, and evaluation under strict governance. 3 · AI Research Scientist, generalizing hardware engineering Transfer and generalization across SoC programs, flows, and toolchains; metareasoning and long-horizon rewards. 4 · AI Research Engineer, RL infra Distributed RL training systems, rollout data planes, and researcher-facing infrastructure at scale.
